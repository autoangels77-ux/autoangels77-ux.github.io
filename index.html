Pricing & Payments

Call-up charges are quoted based on the customer’s location and vehicle collection requirements. Concierge service fees are quoted separately based on the estimated time, coordination and complexity involved in managing the requested repair or service. Workshop payments are settled directly by the customer via online transfer. If repairs extend across multiple days, revisit fees may apply depending on distance and inspection requirements.

FAQs

  • Vehicles should have valid comprehensive insurance cover.
  • Customers may choose their preferred workshop within Colombo and suburbs.
  • Auto Angels fees can be paid by cash or online transfer.
  • Repair updates can be provided via WhatsApp or video call.

Important Note

Auto Angels does not undertake spare-part sourcing or purchasing.

Customers must ensure sufficient fuel is available in the vehicle at handover.

How Auto Angels Works

Simple, transparent and convenient from start to finish.

1. Vehicle Handover

Your vehicle is handed over at your home, office or another convenient location.

2. Initial Inspection

A quick inspection is carried out and repair requirements are discussed.

3. Workshop Coordination

Your vehicle is taken to your preferred workshop or an Auto Angels partner workshop.

4. Repair Updates

Customers are updated on repair costs and timelines before work proceeds.

5. Vehicle Return

Once repairs are completed, the vehicle is returned safely back to the customer.
